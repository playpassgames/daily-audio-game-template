Welcome to Daily Song!

This is an example of how to use the Playpass SDK to build a simple daily audio guessing game.

Each day, you have the opportunity to listen to samples from a song and try to figure out the name. Share your results with a friend and see if they can solve it!

You may also test your complete knowledge by seeing how many in a row you can guess correctly!
The starting time for each hint is random.
Memorizing just the intros won't be enough!
